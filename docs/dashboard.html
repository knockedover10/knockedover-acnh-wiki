📅 Day-by-Day Checklist
Day 0 Island Setup & Opening Ceremony ▼

Starting villagers are a Jock + Sisterly pair. The Jock gives you a hat; the Sisterly gives you a Leaf Umbrella DIY.

Day 1 NookPhone, Pay Loan, DIY Workshop, Museum Tent ▼

Put tools & clothing in your tent — they count as furniture for the "10 items inside" milestone.

Day 2 Vaulting Pole, Shovel, Gather Resources, Nook's Cranny ▼

Stand between two rocks and dig pitfall holes in the squares behind you — prevents you from being knocked back and maximises materials per rock.

Day 3 Bridge, 3 Housing Plots, Furniture, Ladder Recipe ▼
Day 4 Museum Opens, First Villager Moves In, Customisation Workshop ▼
Day 5 Peppy Villager Moves In, Decorate ▼
Day 6 Normal Villager Moves In, Prepare for Upgrade ▼
Day 7 Resident Services Under Maintenance — Keep Decorating ▼
Day 8 Resident Services Opens, Isabelle Arrives, Campsite ▼
Day 9 Campsite Finished — Decorate & Plant ▼
Day 10 First Campsite Visitor, Invite Smug Villager, New Plots ▼

You need 8 villagers total for 3 stars. As of Day 10 you may have 4–5. Two more plots placed now = faster arrival.

Day 11 Campsite Villager Moves In — Continue Villager Hunting ▼
Day 12 Villager 7 Moves In — Unlock Island Evaluations & Push Rating ▼

Isabelle's tip "Not enough bridges/inclines" = each bridge/incline gives a big development boost. Consider adding another.

Day 13 Villager 8 Moves In — Big Rating Push ▼

Spread items across the whole island — scoring is per-acre. Items clustered in one area don't help as much as spread evenly.

Day 14 🎵 KK Concert & Island Designer Unlocked ▼

KK will now visit every Saturday/Sunday. The real island design begins here — time to build that Japanese paradise.
