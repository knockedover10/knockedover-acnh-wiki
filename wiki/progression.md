## Main Early-Game Unlock Order

These are the key unlocks to prioritise in the first 1–2 weeks. Order matters — rushing ahead without completing prerequisites will stall progress.

1. **Day 1–2:** Pay off tent fee (2,000 Nook Miles) → upgrade to house
2. **Day 2–3:** Collect fruit, fish, bugs → donate to Tom Nook for Blathers
3. **Day 3:** Museum tent opens → begin donating (fish, bugs, fossils, art later)
4. **Day 3–4:** Invite 2 new villagers via Nook Miles Ticket (NMT) → they arrive Day 5–6
5. **Day 4–5:** Nook's Cranny unlocks after building the bridge and 3 plots
6. **Day 5+:** Start earning Bells and Nook Miles aggressively for terraforming later
7. **Day 6–7:** Mable visits → repeat visits → Able Sisters shop unlocks
8. **Day 7–8:** Begin working toward 3-star island rating criteria
9. **Post K.K.:** Happy Home Paradise and serious terraforming begins

**Key gate:** K.K. Slider's first concert only happens after Isabelle arrives and the island reaches 3 stars. Everything else is downstream of this.

---

## 3-Star Goals

To reach 3 stars, Isabelle evaluates:

- **Villager count:** Need at least 7–10 villagers on island
- **Furniture placed outdoors:** More is better — aim for 50+ outdoor items
- **Fencing:** Place fencing around the island (counts toward rating)
- **Flowers:** Plant and maintain a variety of flowers, including hybrids
- **Trees:** Have a good spread of fruit, cedar, and hardwood trees
- **Paths:** Not strictly required but contributes to the "polished" look

**Avoid:** Dropping too many items on the ground — clutter hurts the rating.
