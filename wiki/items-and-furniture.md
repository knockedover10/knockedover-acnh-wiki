## Fences

Fencing contributes to the 3-star rating. These styles suit the Japanese theme.

| Fence | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| **Bamboo fence** | DIY — most on-theme; use liberally |
| **Log fence** | Earthy, rustic — suits the graveyard or grove edges |
| **Stone fence** | Structured — good for borders near the shrine |
| **Hedge** | Natural — use for softer district edges |

Avoid: White picket, Wooden-plank, Simple, or any "cute" fence styles.

## Bridges and Inclines

Plan these before placing house plots or terraforming.

| Type | Aesthetic | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| **Log bridge** | Rustic | Best fit for bamboo grove crossings |
| **Stone bridge** | Classic | Shrine district and main paths |
| **Suspension bridge** | Modern | Avoid — clashes with the theme |
| **Wooden bridge** | Neutral | Acceptable for secondary paths |
| **Log/stone inclines** | Natural | Prefer over brick or iron for this theme |
